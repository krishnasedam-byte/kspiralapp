Delete Your Spiral Portfolio Account & Data

Last updated: 20 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your Spiral Portfolio account and the data associated with it. It applies to the app with package name com.spiralportfolio.app.

How to request account deletion

Email krishnasedam@gmail.com from the email address you registered with, using the subject line "Delete my account".

We will delete your account and any associated data within 30 days of the request and confirm by email.

What data is deleted

  • Your account: the email address and login used to sign in are permanently removed from our authentication provider (Supabase).
  • Portfolio data on your device: your holdings, GTT orders, watchlist and settings are stored locally on your device. This data is removed immediately when you uninstall the app or clear the app's storage (Android Settings → Apps → Spiral Portfolio → Storage → Clear data). You do not need to email us to remove on-device data.

What data is retained

We do not retain any personal data after your account is deleted, except where required by law (which, for this app, is none). We do not sell personal data or use advertising or tracking.

Contact

For any questions about deleting your account or data, contact krishnasedam@gmail.com.

See also our Privacy Policy.
